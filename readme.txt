*For WhatsApp Business,
The method only works to transfer chats from
Phone to phone, but only on phones with Android 11 or higher, otherwise it will not work,
WhatsApp Business must be installed, updated, and work normally on the old phone, and the phone must be connected to the Internet.
You must open WhatsApp Business on the old phone, go to the settings, then make a backup copy.
Then connect the old phone to the computer, open the tool, select the option with conversations, and press Backup.
Then you must uninstall WhatsApp Business on the new phone and install a recent version in order for the conversations to be transferred.
Then disconnect the old phone, connect the new one to the computer, open the tool, then select the option with conversations and press Restore
Enter the number and request the code from WhatsApp Business on the new phone, then the code will come either on the old phone or on the SIM card.
After entering the code, click Recover Messages
